SLOTH

Two-toed furry species that lives in the forest and can be home to many other insects such as moths, beetles, cockroaches, fungi and algae.

  • Habitat:Tropical forests of Central and South America.
  • Lifespan:40 years
  • Diet:Cecropia, Insects, leaves and Fruits
  • Species: related to anteaters and armadillos

Sloths are adorable, slow moving mammals that spend most of thier time in trees. These tiny furry mammals live up to 40 years and move long the ground at just 2 meters per minute!
